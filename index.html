About DunamisLAN

Welcome to DunamisLAN, where we bring together gamers from the Dunamis Nexus community for unforgettable LAN party experiences. Whether you're into competitive esports, casual gaming, or just hanging out with fellow gamers, our events are designed to build community through shared passion for gaming.

Our LAN parties feature a welcoming atmosphere for players of all skill levels. Join us as we celebrate gaming, friendship, and faith-based community.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring?

Bring your gaming PC/console, peripherals (keyboard, mouse, headset), power strips, ethernet cable, and any games you want to play. We'll provide tables, chairs, and internet connectivity.

Is food provided?

This varies by event. Check the event details when you register. Some events include meals, others are BYOF (Bring Your Own Food).

What games will be played?

We feature a mix of popular competitive games for tournaments and open gaming for any titles you want to play. Tournament games are announced with each event registration.

Do I need to be part of Dunamis Nexus?

While our events are hosted by Dunamis Nexus, everyone is welcome! We encourage you to join our community on Discord and our forum to stay connected.

What's the age requirement?

Age requirements vary by event and venue. Check the specific event details when registering. Minors may require parent/guardian permission.

Can I just watch or do I need to play?

Spectators are welcome! Whether you want to compete, play casually, or just hang out and watch, there's a place for you at DunamisLAN.

Event Rules & Guidelines

Code of Conduct

  • Treat all participants with respect and kindness
  • No harassment, bullying, or discriminatory behavior
  • Keep language and content appropriate for all ages
  • Follow venue rules and staff instructions
  • Report any concerns to event organizers immediately

Gaming Rules

  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting in any games
  • Tournament participants must check in 15 minutes before their match
  • Matches use default tournament settings unless specified
  • Organizer decisions are final in disputes
  • Spectators must not interfere with active gameplay

Equipment & Setup

  • Label all your equipment with your name
  • You are responsible for your own gear - we are not liable for damage or theft
  • Keep your area clean and organized
  • Do not unplug or interfere with others' equipment
  • Network bandwidth limits may apply - no torrenting or streaming uploads

Safety & Venue

  • Keep walkways and emergency exits clear
  • No smoking, vaping, or alcohol on premises
  • Food and drinks must be kept away from gaming equipment
  • Follow all fire safety and emergency procedures
  • Violation of rules may result in removal from the event
